# Cognitive Layer Definitions
The AI Reasoning Architecture is partitioned into seven distinct layers to ensure separation of concerns:

- **Layer 1 (Ingestion):** Sanitization, PII scrubbing, and raw text to atomized claim parsing.
- **Layer 2 (Structuring):** Semantic mapping and node instantiation.
- **Layer 3 (Consistency):** CCP enforcement (Contradiction Detection & Quarantine).
- **Layer 4 (Inference):** Deductive, Inductive, and Abductive engines.
- **Layer 5 (World Model):** Physical laws, temporal dynamics, and human agent emotional vectors.
- **Layer 6 (Meta-Cognition):** Epistemic horizon mapping and self-reflection.
- **Layer 7 (Oversight):** VCL alignment and final safety constraint validation.
